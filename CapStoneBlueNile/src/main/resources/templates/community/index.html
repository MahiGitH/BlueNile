Outreach

Our Community

While authentic and traditional food is a huge part of our restaurant, we also focus on other activities that will allow us to reach out to the local habesha community, here in Charlotte, North Carolina. Our work consists of sponsoring and catering food to local organizations, such as the annual Ethiopian Celebration. We also allow for young kids to complete their volunteer hours by helping out at the restaurant. This teaches them how to prepare for future jobs and the responsibilies that come witt starting your own business.
